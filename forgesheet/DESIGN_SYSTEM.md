# Design System

## Visual Direction

ForgeSheet blends parchment, brass, and obsidian tones with modern panel layouts and restrained fantasy ornament.

## Tokens

- Ember: `#c96f32`
- Brass: `#9c7b3c`
- Soot: `#17181d`
- Coal: `#23262e`
- Parchment: `#f5efdf`
- Sky: `#94b3c8`
- Rune: `#7f9a89`

## Typography

- Display: classic serif stack for fantasy flavor
- UI: clean modern sans stack for high readability

## Components

- `Panel` for major surfaces
- `PanelSoft` for nested surfaces
- `Button` for consistent controls
- `Badge` for status and metadata

See `design/` for mood, wireframes, prompts, and diagrams.
