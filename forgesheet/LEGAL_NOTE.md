# Legal Note

PHB-Complete Engine is designed to avoid redistributing copyrighted book prose.

It ships:

- ids
- source references
- neutral summaries
- structured mechanics placeholders
- completeness tracking

It does not ship:

- copied spell descriptions
- class feature prose from proprietary books
- book passages from PHB, DMG, or MM

The owner of the books may add their own text locally through the user-content workflow.
